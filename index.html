WHO WE ARE

The idea for Munawwar, Nazahath & Co started when we were working as lawyers in the Maldives Inland Revenue Authority (MIRA), representing MIRA at the Tribunals and Courts. During our time, in and out of court, we saw a lot of individuals, businesses sitting across the table who were massively under-serviced, despite the growing legal industry in the Maldives.

We have worked in both public and private sector. Whether it is government bureaucracy, navigating the legal system, lack of proper disclosure and consent, or absence of effective communication - we understand that navigating these challenges can be crucial to your success and we are here to help.

By empowering our clients with our custom-built portal that helps them to access and monitor progress of our work in real time, we are adopting modern technology with our core values and challenging our clients to re-think the traditional law firm. Our take on the modern law firm involves a setting where the regular public in general will be able to access the legal information they require at the click of a few buttons, whenever and wherever they are. With our user-friendly bots that are available 24/7, to our podcasts, we aspire to make legal services accessible to all.

We share your passion, commitment and dedication in the pursuit of your success.
