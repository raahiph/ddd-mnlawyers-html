PRACTICE AREAS

TAX ADVISORY

Our firm understands the tax regime of the Maldives. Our firm is well-versed in Tax Registration, Income Tax, Goods and Services Tax, Withholding Tax, Pay As you Earn (PAYE) system, Objection and Appeal process, Transfer Pricing, Tax Controversies, Tax Planning and Criminal Prosecutions with regard to Tax Crimes and other complex tax issues.

BANKING & FINANCE 

Our range of Banking and Finance related services includes, advising both onshore and offshore clients on Maldivian banking laws and regulations. The scope of our services also includes a wide range of banking and financial issues such as financing, refinancing facility, loan, guarantee and foreclosure.

TOURISM & HOSPITALITY 

We offer legal services in the tourism industry. In particular, we advise on the registration of foreign investments, acquisitions and transfer of property, lease arrangements, labor and regulatory compliance.

LAND AND PROPERTY 

We advise on all aspects of land and property such as acquisition and development of property, sale and purchase of property, transfer and lease of property. We also provide legal advice and litigation service on inheritance, gifting, wills and strata title.

EMPLOYMENT

The firm advises its clients, including legal representation at the Employment Tribunal and Courts, on the legal aspects of employment, termination of employment, dismissal of employees, layoff and obligations under Maldives Pension Act to ensure full adherence to the Employment laws. We also draft and review internal policies and employment contracts and provide in-house training on labor related issues.

BUSINESS SETUP 

We assist our clients with the initial setup of their business and to create a corporate identity by assisting with trademark and copyright matters. Our services includes, providing assistance with business registration application, drafting and reviewing Partnership or Shareholder Agreements and other related documents.

BUSINESS LIQUIDATION 

We assist our clients through the liquidation process by taking on the tasks of a liquidator, such as realization of assets, identification of liabilities, paying off debts, cancellation of contracts and closing down or deregistration of the business.

LEGAL DRAFTING 

We provide a versatile range of drafting services in all the areas of law, from drafting of Agreements, Deeds, Conveyancing and drafting of court pleadings.

REGULATORY COMPLIANCE 

We provide advice on complex regulatory matters and help our clients to comply with laws, regulations, policies and procedures and stay up-to date with the regulatory changes and developments in their respective industries. We also provide in-house training on compliance related issues.

LITIGATION & DISPUTE RESOLUTION 

We specialize in reliable legal solutions including; Negotiation, Alternative Dispute Resolution and Litigation. From ordinary small claim cases to complex cases, our firm provides the same dedication and qualified representation to all our clients.
